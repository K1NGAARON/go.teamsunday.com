Warehousing:

Warehousing your items in an organized & transparent way takes time and resources. This cost includes:

  • Physical Warehouse location
  • Insurance
  • Time for stock management
  • Warehousing software
  • Packing stations
  • Packing materials

Wardrobe Platform:

Transparent monthly fee including all software and warehousing (incl. insurance). The best tool to keep a clear overview on your stock levels and shipments.

Warehousing is our core business for hundreds of customers. We are able to split costs amongst our clients so you can benefit from our scale advantage.

Fulfillment:

Most companies give the responsibility of the package's fulfillment as an extra to their current staff. The hassle of these shipments include:

  • Picking the items
  • Packing them in the right boxes
  • Creating shipping labels
  • Writing personalized notes
  • Closing & taping boxes & Bringing them to a parcel point
  • Managing customs administration

Wardrobe Fulfillment:

Sunday can drastically reduce your fulfillment cost in 3 ways:

  • We have trained staff and proven processes in place to work as efficiently as possible
  • We invest heavily in software automation of these processes to reduce human time spent
  • It’s what we do

Data management:

One of the biggest time drainers is actually gathering the correct and complete information for shipments (from addresses to sizes).

The responsible person needs to:

  • Request, enter & update the information in the chose system
  • Keep all stakeholders op to date with tracking information
  • Manually create every shipping label, make sure the correct sizes & notes are put in the correct boxes

Data management Wardrobe:

The platform still requires the user to enter the shipping data in the system. This however is way faster with features like:

  • Bulk shipment import
  • Input form where the receiver can enter their own data
  • Integrations like Hubspot, Shopify, Zendesk,....
  • Stock management, Track & Traces and campaign data is all managed automatically in the platform

Error management:

Managing mistakes is a huge hidden cost. Mistakes happen, wrong items shipped, request for items with no stock, wrong address, parcel service loses the package. All these things happen on a regular basis when managing shipments yourself. Someone is easily spending a couple of hours rectifying a lost shipment.

Error management Wardrobe:

Our team is ready to help you solve any issue. Did the carrier lose your package? We’ll be the ones waiting in the helpdesk line. A return? Our team processes it and adds it back to your stock. Zero hassle. And always a real person to talk to and help you out.
